Let's Start

For Pizza Base

Firstly, in a large mixing bowl take warm water, sugar and dry yeast and give a quick mix. Allow it to rest for 5 minutes. Now add all purpose flour, salt and olive oil. Knead the dough adding water as required. Tuck the dough and form to a ball. Place in a large mixing bowl. Cover rest and rise in a warm place for about 2 hours. Punch the dough with fist to release down the air.

Preparing the Pizza

Place the dough on dusted pizza plate or tray.Flatten the dough by stretching with both hands. Further, leaving a cm or more and create a dent. Then prick with centre of dough with the help of fork. Furthermore, spread pizza sauce generously leaving the sides slightly. Also top with topping of choice. Then spread grated cheese and sprinkle italian seasonings.

Baking time!!

Further, bake at 500 degrees fahrenheit for about 12 - 15 minutes.Sprinkle over some oregano and chilli fakes. Finally, slice and serve pizza hot!!!
